What We Offer

Signature Standard Clean

Reliable upkeep for weekly, fortnightly, or monthly cleaning. We use non-toxic, biodegradable products, HEPA-filter vacuums, and microfibre cloths to leave your home fresh, healthy, and safe for children, pets, and allergy-sensitive households.

Detailed Deep Clean

Comprehensive top-to-bottom refresh for seasonal cleans, post-illness recovery, moving in, or special occasions. Extra attention to detail, hard-to-reach spots, and thorough allergen reduction for a healthier home environment.

Cleaner Home, Healthier Family

Non-toxic, allergy-safe cleaning for your family

At Eira Cleaning, we use eco-friendly, fragrance-free products, HEPA vacuums, and microfibre cloths to create a healthier home for your family. Our allergy-safe approach helps reduce dust, pet dander, mould, and chemical residues—common triggers for asthma, eczema, and sensitivities.

With carefully trained two-person teams, we clean with precision and care—minimising disruption while delivering premium results you can trust.
